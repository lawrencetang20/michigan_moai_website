Our Mission

We bridge language and cultural gaps by connecting AANHPI victims of hate with therapists who speak their language and understand their experience.

Trauma Support

Therapy sessions tailored for hate crime survivors.

Multi-State Reach

Leveraging PSYPACT to serve clients across 42 states.

Peer Mentorship

Training AAPI youth to support one another.
