Michigan Legal Services Directory

This directory is a compiled document listing legal service providers across Michigan who offer culturally competent and, when available, bilingual support for Asian American, Native Hawaiian, and Pacific Islander (AANHPI) individuals. It includes organizations and attorneys experienced in assisting victims of hate crimes, discrimination, and racial bias, as well as those navigating immigration, civil rights, and other legal challenges. The directory is intended as a referral tool for advocates and community members seeking accessible, culturally informed legal assistance within Michigan.
