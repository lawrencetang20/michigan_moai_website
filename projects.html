Our Projects

Healing from Hate Project

We provide culturally-competent therapy referrals and fund sessions for AANHPI hate crime survivors in their native language, reducing barriers to care.

P2P Mentorship Training

AAPI high school students receive 1–2 day training from U-Michigan’s Eisenberg Center to become peer mentors—fostering emotional support and reducing stigma statewide.
