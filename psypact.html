PSYPACT Lab

PSYPACT is an interstate compact among 42 states that permits licensed psychologists to practice telehealth across state lines. Michigan Moai leverages PSYPACT to expand our referral network, ensuring AANHPI clients can access culturally competent teletherapy even if their provider is located in another member state.

  • Compact of 42 states (more pending)
  • Telehealth practice across state lines
  • Used to refer clients to out-of-state providers
  • Grant funds cover out-of-state session costs not covered by insurance
